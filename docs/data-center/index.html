    Data Centers

    What are data centers ?

    In the advent of cloud computing, companies are preparing to clear land to make room for warehouses. These warehouses are made to house the worlds computing power. Companies rely on these warehouses and their performance to ensure the best services provided to their customers. These warehouses are running software that is responsible for streaming services and other business offerings. However, these warehouse use a lot of energy and this essay plans on showing how different forms of media try to raise awareness. Awareness, that data centers are damaging the Eco-system and contributing to climate change.
